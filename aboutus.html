Who do We Cater?

USA - Land of immigrants with deep rooted cultures spanning across length and width of entire World. “Desi Chowrastha” is here, to celebrate culture, heritage through food, much more than nostalgia and recipes. In today’s ever-growing competitive market, we are here with delicacies that tantalize the taste buds, providing excellent customer service and value for money. As Sam Walton said,“there is only one boss, the customer” . We think “Customers certify the success of recipe, the demand and growth”.

The USA has the largest number of immigrants from all over the world. The Indian migrant population has been exploring in the last 56- years. With market research, and proven industry experience in creating a brand and making it a landmark for hangout, Sharath Podduturi is on his way to launch “Desi Chowrastha” with a vision to see “Chowrastha” in every city of USA for people…..

We are on a pursuit to create a one stop shop for groceries, halal meat and restaurant concept that caters not only to the largest Indian population in Ashburn , VA, but to everyone that is open to explore to the food of complex flavors and spices; also understand years of evolution and the prominent culture that is ingrained in the food.

“Desi Chowrastha” gives attention to the diversified food and culture of various cities in India. With the touch of southern spices, we are trying to deliver “mouth licking scrumptious food” catering to Indians, and local and international foodies.

Desi - Land, Country

Word used to describe the people, cultures, and products of the Indian subcontinent and their diaspora. Desi traces its origin specifically to the people of the countries India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.

Chowrastha - the intersection of four streets

The word is used in causal way to communicate about the joint to hang out with friends and how Indian culture evolved in every city around these so called “Chowrastha’s”

The logo is inspired by intersection of 4 streets. Letters “DC” are designed to show the fun feeling of life with a smile.

Vision
One stop shop catering to South Asian community with groceries, halal meat and mouthwatering food, that tantalize the taste buds.
Mission
  • To provide One stop shop that caters to the needs of South Asian community.
  • To provide refined environment and experience for people to come together and celebrate the flavors of food.
  • To develop a model that can be replicated in every city of USA and being back the culture of “Chowrastha” and Indianness.
